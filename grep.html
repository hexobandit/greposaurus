About

For two decades, our talented troupe of Lemmur monkeys has been crafting visual stories that resonate across species and cultures. Based in the heart of Prague, our passionate primate production team brings their natural creativity and agility to transforming concepts into compelling cinematic experiences.


Every frame tells a story. Every project is a jungle expedition. We don't just produce videos – our Lemmur crew creates memories that swing from heart to heart, lasting generations.

Our talented Lemmur monkeys craft professional corporate videos that swing your brand message straight to the hearts of your audience. With their keen eye for detail and natural agility, they create compelling narratives that communicate your company's essence with unmatched creativity.
